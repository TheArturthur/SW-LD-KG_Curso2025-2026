Dataset Requirements Evaluation

Dataset: Air Quality in Madrid

Source: Madrid Open Data Portal

R1. Smart City domain

✅ Yes. The dataset belongs to the Environment domain, specifically Air Quality.

R2. CSV format

✅ Yes. The dataset is available as a CSV file.

R3. Open license

✅ Yes. The dataset is released under CC BY 4.0, allowing reuse and publication with attribution.

R4. Linkable with generic entities

✅ Yes. It contains geographical coordinates and timestamps, enabling links to GeoNames and W3C Time vocabularies.

R5. Documentation (optional)

✅ Yes. The open data portal provides metadata and field descriptions.

R6. Multiple sources (optional)

⚠️ Partially. The data comes from a single source but could be enriched with AEMET or Eurostat datasets.
